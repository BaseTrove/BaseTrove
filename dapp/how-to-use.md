# 📱 How to Use the DApp

The BaseTrove DApp is your direct interface with the Vault. Sell $TROVE at the guaranteed floor price, monitor the protocol's real-time stats, and verify every claim the team makes.

---

## Accessing the DApp

> **URL:** [basetrove.io/dapp](https://basetrove.io/dapp) *(published at launch)*

Runs directly in your browser. No downloads, no installs.

---

## Requirements

- A Web3 wallet with Base network support (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, Rabby, etc.)
- $TROVE tokens in your wallet
- A small amount of ETH on Base for gas (fractions of a cent)

---

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|

Or visit [chainlist.org](https://chainlist.org) and search "Base" to add in one click.

---

## Selling $TROVE to the Vault

1. **Connect your wallet** — click "Connect Wallet" and approve
2. **Check the floor price** — displayed on the DApp. This is the ETH you receive per TROVE
3. **Enter the amount** — type how many TROVE to sell, or click "MAX"
4. **Review the output** — DApp shows exact ETH received before you confirm
5. **Approve** — first-time users approve the TROVE contract once (tiny gas cost)
6. **Confirm** — sign the transaction. TROVE is burned, ETH lands in your wallet instantly

**Zero fees on Vault sales.** What you see is what you get.

## DApp Dashboard

Live on-chain stats — no backend, no manipulation:

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| **Vault ETH** | Total ETH held in the Vault |
| **Floor Price** | Current guaranteed redemption price (ETH per TROVE) |
| **Circulating Supply** | TROVE tokens currently in circulation |
| **Total Burned** | All-time permanent burns |
| **Last Buyback** | Amount and timestamp of last mining buyback |
| **Mining Wallet Balance** | Live BTC + ETH balance of the buyback wallet |
| **Daily Vault Growth** | ETH added to Vault in last 24 hours |
