## Phase 3 — The Warehouse

The long-term mining vision is a dedicated warehouse facility housing as many ASIC miners as possible — a large-scale, permanent mining operation that generates substantial daily revenue flowing into the Vault and the TROVE ecosystem continuously.

- [ ] Dedicated mining facility secured
- [ ] Large-scale ASIC deployment — scale determined by protocol revenue
- [ ] Industrial solar and battery infrastructure
- [ ] Revenue output scales with hardware — floor growth accelerates proportionally
- [ ] Full on-chain transparency — every unit's revenue traceable

---

## Phase 4 — Ecosystem Revenue Streams

The protocol is designed to incorporate additional revenue sources over time. Every new stream feeds the same flywheel — more ETH into the Vault, more buy pressure on TROVE. Planned additions include:

- [ ] **Automated trading bots** — protocol-run bots generating trading revenue directed to the Vault
- [ ] **Ecosystem partnerships** — revenue-sharing agreements with Base ecosystem projects
- [ ] **Games & interactive products** — onchain games and applications where fees flow back to the Vault
- [ ] **Additional yield strategies** — Vault ETH deployed into low-risk yield where appropriate

Each addition compounds on top of the existing mining base. The warehouse produces the floor. Everything else accelerates it.
