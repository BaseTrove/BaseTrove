# ❓ FAQ

---

### What is $TROVE?

$TROVE is the native token of BaseTrove — a deflationary ERC-20 on Base chain backed by a Vault of ETH and fueled by solar-powered ASIC miners running 24/7.

---

### What backs the price floor?

The Vault — a smart contract holding ETH. The floor price equals total Vault ETH divided by circulating $TROVE supply. Guaranteed by code, not promises.

---

### Can the floor price go down?

No. The Vault ETH only increases (from swap fees and mining revenue). The supply only decreases (from burns). Both movements push the floor price upward.

The floor stays flat only if zero ETH enters the Vault and zero burns occur simultaneously — effectively impossible while miners are running and trading is happening.

---

### What happens if the DEX price falls below the Vault floor?

Rational traders buy TROVE cheaply on the DEX and sell it to the Vault at the guaranteed floor price, pocketing the ETH difference. This closes the gap automatically. Those tokens are permanently burned in the process, raising the floor for everyone remaining.

---

### What coin do the miners mine?

The miners produce whichever Proof-of-Work coin is most profitable at any given time — Bitcoin, Zcash, Ethereum Classic, or others. The team monitors profitability and can switch algorithms to maximize revenue flowing into the Vault and buybacks. All mined coins are converted to ETH on Base before deployment.

---

### How do I know the miners are actually running?

The buyback wallet is public. You can watch mining revenue arrive, get converted to ETH on Base, and get split between the Vault and buybacks — all verifiable on-chain. The live dashboard also shows hash rate, daily revenue, and every transaction. No trust required.

---

### Is there a team token dump risk?

No. There are zero team tokens. The team holds no $TROVE at launch — not vested, not locked, not anywhere. The team earns revenue exclusively through the 50% development share of swap fees. No token dump is possible.

---

### What is the swap fee?

Between 2% and 5%, adjusted manually by the team based on market conditions. Half goes to the Vault, half to the team development fund (reinvested into more mining hardware and operations). The fee is always visible on-chain.

---

### Can the fee change without warning?

The fee is adjustable within a range enforced by the smart contract. Changes are visible on-chain immediately. The team's incentive is to keep fees reasonable — too high and volume drops, reducing both Vault growth and team revenue.

---

### How often do buybacks happen?

As often as mining revenue accumulates — typically daily or more. Base chain's sub-cent gas costs mean even small revenue batches can be deployed immediately rather than waiting to batch up.

---

### Where can I trade $TROVE?

On Uniswap v2 on Base via two pools: TROVE/USDC and TROVE/ETH. You can also sell directly to the Vault via the DApp at the floor price with zero fees.

---

### Is this a rug pull?

The rug pull checklist: anonymous team, no liquidity lock, team tokens at launch, no audit, no transparency.

BaseTrove: community-known team, locked liquidity, zero team tokens, published audit, fully public on-chain operations. We can't force trust — we can only make verification easy. DYOR.

---

### What chain is BaseTrove on?

Base — Coinbase's Ethereum L2. Chain ID: 8453.
