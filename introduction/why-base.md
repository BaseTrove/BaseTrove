# Why Base Chain?

BaseTrove is deployed on **Base** — Coinbase's Ethereum L2 — and the choice was deliberate.

---

## Low Fees Enable High Frequency

The buyback engine only works if it actually runs. On Ethereum mainnet, a single buyback transaction can cost $20–$80 in gas. That's a meaningful drag on the mining revenue that should be compounding for holders.

On Base, the same transaction costs **fractions of a cent.**

This means:
- Buybacks can happen **daily or multiple times per day** rather than weekly
- Smaller mining revenue batches can be deployed immediately instead of queuing up
- The Vault accumulates ETH faster, raising the price floor more frequently

Low fees turn the flywheel from a slow grind into a fast spin.

---

## Ethereum Security, Coinbase Distribution

Base is an Ethereum L2 — it inherits Ethereum's security model while settling on the most battle-tested blockchain in DeFi. Your $TROVE is as secure as anything on Ethereum mainnet.

Base also benefits from Coinbase's enormous distribution. As one of the most widely-used crypto platforms, Coinbase has a direct on-ramp to Base that millions of users can access — widening the potential audience for $TROVE without requiring a bridge.

---

## Deep, Native Liquidity

Base hosts deep, battle-tested DEX infrastructure including Uniswap v3 and Aerodrome Finance — the protocols where $TROVE's two liquidity pools live. This means:

- Tight spreads for arbitrage bots
- Reliable price discovery
- No need to seed liquidity on an obscure chain with thin volume
