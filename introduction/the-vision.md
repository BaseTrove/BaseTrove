# The Vision

Most crypto projects promise a deflationary model — then rely entirely on community hype and trading volume to sustain it. When the hype dies, the burns stop. The floor collapses.

**BaseTrove is built differently.**

---

## A Token Backed by Real Energy

At the heart of BaseTrove is a fleet of **solar-powered ASIC miners** — physical hardware converting sunlight into Bitcoin, operating around the clock, every day of the year. No team wallets funding operations. No "marketing buys." Just machines, sun, and math.

The revenue generated by these miners flows automatically into the BaseTrove ecosystem:

- **Half goes directly into the Vault** — permanently raising the ETH price floor for every $TROVE holder.
- **Half executes buybacks** — purchasing $TROVE from the open market and burning it forever, continuously reducing supply.

This creates something genuinely rare in DeFi: a **deflationary mechanism that operates independently of market sentiment.**

## The End State

Imagine a token where:

- The supply only ever decreases
- The ETH backing each token only ever increases
- Miners buy and burn tokens 24/7, independent of market conditions
- The energy powering all of this comes from the sun

That's $TROVE.
